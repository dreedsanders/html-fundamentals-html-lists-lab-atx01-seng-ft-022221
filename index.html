Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
    • mozzarella
    • pepper chack
  • 1 tbsp of butter

Steps to making a basic grilled cheese

  1. Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. Place bread in frying pan and frying
  3. Add cheese on top of bread
  4. Cover with second slice of bread
  5. Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
